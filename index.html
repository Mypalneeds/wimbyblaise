Wimbyblaise — Free Online Note Taker, Sketch Board and Ideation Tool

The best free note-taking app available in your browser — no sign-up required

Wimbyblaise (whatimeant) is a free, open-source online note-taking application, digital sketchpad, and brainstorming ideation board. It is the ideal free notepad for students, professionals, writers, designers, and anyone who needs to capture ideas quickly. Unlike other online note apps, Wimbyblaise requires zero registration and works entirely in your browser.

Free Note Taking Features

Take notes with our free rich-text editor. Format your notes with bold, italic, underline, headings, bullet lists, numbered lists, blockquotes, checklists, and custom text colors. Manage multiple notes with our sidebar. Auto-save keeps your notes safe in your browser automatically. Export your notes to PDF, Word document, plain text, or Markdown with one click. Wimbyblaise is the best free online notepad for students, a free note taking app for work, and a free digital notebook for anyone.

Free Sketchpad and Drawing Tool

Use our free online sketchpad to draw freehand sketches, diagrams, wireframes, and doodles. Choose from pen, brush, marker, eraser, line, rectangle, circle, and arrow tools. Adjust brush size and opacity. Undo strokes and change colors. Export your sketch as PNG or JPG image, or as a PDF. Wimbyblaise is the best free drawing tool online and free sketchbook app with no downloads.

Free Ideation Board and Brainstorming Tool

Brainstorm your best ideas using our free sticky notes ideation board. Add colorful draggable sticky notes in yellow, pink, green, blue, purple, and orange. Organize thoughts visually. Export your ideation board to PDF or plain text. Wimbyblaise is the best free brainstorming app online and free mind mapping alternative with no account needed.

Export to Multiple Formats

Wimbyblaise lets you export your notes, sketches, and ideation boards to: PDF (.pdf), Microsoft Word (.doc), PNG image (.png), JPG image (.jpg), Plain text (.txt), and Markdown (.md). It is the only free note app with this many export options.
